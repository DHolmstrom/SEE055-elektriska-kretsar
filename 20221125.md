#### Resistor

$$u(t)=Ri(t)$$
$$Z_R=R$$
$$U=Z_RI=RI$$

#### Spole

$$u(t)=L{di\over dt}$$
$$Z_L=j\omega t$$
$$U=j\omega L*I$$

#### Kondensator

$$i(t)=C{du\over dt}$$
$${1\over j\omega C}={-j\over \omega C}$$
$$Z_C={1\over j\omega C}={-j\over \omega C}$$
$${U=Z_CI=-{j\over \omega C}I}$$
