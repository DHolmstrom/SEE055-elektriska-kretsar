$$u(t)=ûcos(\omega t+\alpha)=>U=ûe^{j\alpha}$$

$$\varphi=\alpha-\beta$$

$$\$={U*I^*\over 2}={ûe^{j\alpha}îe^{j\beta}\over 2}={û\over\sqrt{2}}e^{j\alpha}{î\over\sqrt{2}}e^{j\beta}=$$
$$=U_eI_ee^{j\varphi}=U_eI_ecos(\varphi)+jU_eI_esin(\varphi)=P+jQ$$
